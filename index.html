Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here is what you should know about data collection on this site.

  • Google Analytics 4 is used to understand how visitors use the tool. It collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time spent, browser type, and approximate location (city-level). No personally identifiable information (name, email, address) is collected.
  • No file uploads. All images and PDFs you process are handled entirely in your browser. They are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server.
  • Local storage is used to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode), extraction history (last 10 items), and analytics consent choice. This data stays on your device.
  • Cookies are set by Google Analytics for tracking purposes. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's partner site policy.

By clicking "Accept", you consent to the use of Google Analytics for anonymous usage measurement. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.
