Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Free Text OCR ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our OCR tool and website at Free Text OCR.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Anonymous Usage Data (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our site. GA4 collects anonymized data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Approximate geographic location (city-level only)
  • Referring website or search engine

No personally identifiable information (name, email, address, IP address) is collected. GA4 is configured with IP anonymization enabled. Data is retained for 14 months and then automatically deleted.

You may opt out of GA4 tracking at any time by clicking "Decline" in the privacy modal or by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

1.2 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests. These cookies collect data about:

  • Your visits to this site and other websites
  • Your interaction with ads (clicks, impressions)
  • Non-personally identifying information such as browser type and device

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google's Advertising Policies
  • Google Ads Settings
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out

To manage your cookie preferences for this site, use our Cookie Settings panel.

1.3 No File Uploads

All image and PDF processing happens entirely in your browser. Files you select are never uploaded, stored, transmitted, or shared with any server. The OCR engine (Tesseract.js) runs locally on your device. This is a core design principle of our service.

1.4 Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to remember:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Extraction history (last 10 items, stored locally)
  • Analytics consent (whether you accepted or declined GA4 tracking)
  • Cookie consent (your advertising cookie preferences)

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

2. Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the site to function properly. They enable core functionality such as remembering your theme preference and consent choices.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

Google Analytics 4 sets cookies to distinguish unique users and track usage patterns. These cookies are only set if you click "Accept" in our privacy consent modal.

2.3 Advertising Cookies

Google AdSense may set cookies to serve personalized advertisements. These cookies track your browsing across websites to build a profile of your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising as described in Section 1.2 above.

2.4 Third-Party Cookies

The following third parties may also set cookies on this site:

  • Google (Analytics & AdSense) — Google Privacy Policy
  • jsDelivr (CDN for Tesseract.js) — jsDelivr Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare (CDN for PDF.js) — Cloudflare Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our OCR tool
  • Understand usage patterns and popular features
  • Serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregate data with:

  • Google — through Google Analytics 4 and Google AdSense
  • CDN providers — jsDelivr and Cloudflare serve library files; they may log standard web server information

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Your Rights

5.1 GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing — limit how we use your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Object — object to data processing (including analytics and advertising)

To exercise these rights, contact us. We will respond within 30 days.

5.2 CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Delete personal information we hold about you
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us so we can delete it.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, since all file processing occurs locally in your browser and we collect minimal data, the risk of data breach is inherently low. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the repository's discussions page.

The OCR engine (Tesseract.js) runs entirely in your browser. No file data is ever transmitted over the network. Free Text OCR is a 100% client-side tool.
